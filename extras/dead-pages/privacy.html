Privacy Policy
There are currently no analytics deployed on this website. There are currently no cookies created or used by this site. In the future there may be some data gathered, but it will always be as minimal and as transparent as possible. I'm mostly just interested in the number of visitors and their general geo-location.


Site Policy and Philosophy
This site is designed with simplicity as the ultimate goal. This starts with the source, where clean, well organized pages that have useful comment landmarks are the standard. I also aim to make the pages highly accesible by using standard layout conventions and clear block naming.
Speed is also a priority on this site. Being hosted on GitHub means that server-side technologies like PHP are off the table anyways, but on pages where it is not neccesary JavaScript is also eschewed. While headers and footers may look hard-coded, they are actually pre-syncronysed across pages by a Python script. This keeps the site consistent while being simple to change, and also means that there are no changes required at page load.
